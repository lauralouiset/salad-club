About Salad Club

Salad Club generates healthy salads with the click of a button!

Salad Club was created by Laura-Louise Tobin as a project for the Full-Time Front-End Development Bootcamp at Hacker You. Salad Club was built using JavaScript and Jquery, and SCSS.

Salad ingredient graphics were designed by yours truly in Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop. Salad leaf graphics are from Adobe Stock.

About Salad Club

Salad Club generates healthy salads with the click of a button!

Salad Club was created by Laura-Louise Tobin as a project for Hacker You's Full-Time Front-End Development Bootcamp. Salad Club was built using JavaScript and Jquery,

Salad ingredient graphics were designed in Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop. Salad leaf graphics are from Adobe Stock.
